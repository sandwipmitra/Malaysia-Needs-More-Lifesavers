Malaysia Needs More Lifesavers — Are You In?

We’re Not Just Training Lifeguards — We’re Building a Movement

Together with LSSM, we're creating a safer Malaysia — one certified lifesaver at a time.

Saving Lives Starts with Action

The recent article in The Star is a wake-up call on Malaysia’s silent drowning crisis, especially in hotel and condo pools. Red Rescue is proud to stand with the Malaysia Life Saving Society (LSSM) in tackling this challenge head-on.

  • ✅ Certifying new lifeguards across the country
  • ✅ Training youth & adults with real-world rescue skills
  • ✅ Creating career pathways in lifesaving
  • ✅ Supporting hotels, schools & communities with certified pros

Join the Lifesaving Movement

Collaboration, not competition. Purpose, not profit. At Red Rescue, we’re building a national legacy of lifesaving — and we want you with us.
